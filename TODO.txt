==================================== Boss ======================================

Upon re-entering the unyu village you started in, it is eerily barren of Unyus
and there is also black goop and destruction everywhere.

Then, the unyu you play as sees a black Goop drip from the ceiling onto its
head. It then jumps out of the way as the Boss falls from the top of the screen.
Player regains control and the boss sequence starts.

-- Phase 1 --
Boss mainly jumps around and spews out projectiles the player has to dodge.
There is a water refill tank located in the arena, but it does not act as a
checkpoint; there is no red-colored dot on the center of its sprite. The last
checkpoint will be set to the moment the boss started.

Boss will periodically rest for a moment probably after a very intense Attack
phase it does. This will give the player the chance to damage boss by making a
ladder up to the ceiling and breaking stalactite above the boss. Enemy
projectiles don't remove ice platforms so the player can build this ladder
across multiple rest periods. After one hit boss flees to next room.

-- Phase 2 --
The checkpoint will be set to the start of the room.

Upon entering the room, boss will avoid you and will break the stalactites above
your head using its projectiles. So you need to dodge them all first.

Afterwards enter a sequence where you have to follow the boss as he flees But
there's a bunch of bluxes along the path---probably a new enemy type as well---
shooting at you that you have to avoid/hit. Then maybe you have to do a puzzle
platform section to get to the third room

-- Phase 3 --
This room is outside

upon entering the room, the boss is hidden directly above the entrance. It will
drop down on you once you arrive and start chomping you. But then the Sign Guy
throws a spear at it from off-screen that hits the creature which frees you.
Shortly afterward you get up and must start running away from creature.

The checkpoint will be set at the point the player regains control.

Uhh then you and the sign guy work together (somehow) to lure the big creature
into a pit of water. Like, I guess the creature gets Really Angry and starts
chasing you and the sign guy (the sign guy no longer has a weapon so it has no
reason to fear it). so you have to be do these platforming challenges (perphaps
puzzles too?) with great exigency otherwise you Die.

then you reach a Dead-End but there is a trap for the monster. like The ground
is visibly different in one patch of ground and you can see water underneath.
As the creature quickly approaches, Sign Guy opens up the trap. However the
Creature abruptly stops before Sign Guy shortly before opens the trap, as It was
suspect of the strange patch of ground. So that means the creature did not fall
for the trap. The creature then does something which could be interpreted as
laughing, and then leaps across the pit to Eat You. But then the Sign Guy throws
the empty sign that he was carrying toward the Creature Mouth!! Omg!! The
Creature didn't think about that Sign! What a clutch! The impact stops the

The boss, upon falling into the pit of water, dissolves and dies. And also
all your unyu friends are then freed from its intestinal tract because it

Sign Guy then explains that you can save your friends by using the Secret Orbs
which will melt the ice before they die of hypothermia. Then, the number of
secret orbs you collected equate to how many unyus get saved.

If you collect no secret orbs then the minimum amount of Unyus are saved: some
